Credit Note

The credit note module facilitates the creation, management, and tracking of credit notes issued to customers. These documents serve as formal acknowledgments of adjustments to invoices, such as refunds, discounts, or corrections.

Key functionalities include:

  • Automated Creation: Credit notes can be generated automatically based on returns, cancellations, or invoice corrections.
  • Manual Adjustments: Provides flexibility to create credit notes manually for custom scenarios or ad hoc adjustments.
  • Invoice Linking: Links credit notes directly to the original invoice, ensuring transparency and traceability in financial records.
  • Customizable Formats: Supports customizable credit note templates to comply with legal and organizational requirements.
