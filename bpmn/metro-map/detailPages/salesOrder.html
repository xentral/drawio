Sales Order

The Sales Order is the central document in Xentral that manifests a sale event and serves as the starting point for all subsequent processes such as fulfillment, invoicing, reservations, etc.

Sales Orders can be preceded by Offers, which are non-binding documents that are used to present products and prices to customers.
