Current issues in Delivery Handover

Our delivery handover is currently causing severe performance issues in many customer instances.

A joint task force has been formed between FFU, SPS and SRE analysing root causes and implementing improvements to our logic.

Key steps are:

  • Carve out unnecessary database queries: fetch data once and persist in PHP variables rather than fetching over and over
  • Move non-time critical actions to worker queues instead of doing everything synchronously
  • Indroduce pre-checks to only calculate every sales order when stock is low
  • Introduce stock allocation table which persists previously allocated quantities and allows for quick and cheap updates based on sales order and/or product
