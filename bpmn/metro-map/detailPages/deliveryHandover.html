Delivery Handover

When sales orders are "all-green", i.e. ready to be fulfilled, they are transferred into the logistics processing.

Xentral offers several ways to transfer sales order into the logistics processing:

  • Manual individual handover
  • Manual bulk handover
  • Automamted bulk handover
  • Enhanced automation via picklist profiless

During this step

  • delivery notes and / or invoices are created (configurable)
  • automatic stock retrievals can be performed (configurable)
