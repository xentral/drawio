Traffic Lights

The traffic lights on the sales order serve as gate-keeper which only allows those sales order to pass into the logistics processing which satisfy all conditions for processing.

Stock available: Indicates that all stock items are available in sufficient quantity to fulfil the sales order Stock missing: Indicates that some stock items are missing or only available in insufficient quantity

Postage calculated: Indicates that postage has been calculated. For more details please refer to the Confluence Internal documentation on Sales Order Traffic Lights.

Postage missing: Indicates that NONE of the conditions mentioned above are met
