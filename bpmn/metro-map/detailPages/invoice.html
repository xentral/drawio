Invoice

The invoice is a core financial document generated after the delivery handover process. It serves as the formal record of a sale, detailing the items or services provided, their quantities, and the associated prices.

The creation and management of invoices are subject to configurable financial process settings. Key features and options include:

  • Automatic Generation: Invoices can be created automatically during the delivery handover step based on predefined rules.
  • Customizable Formats: Invoice templates can be tailored to meet legal, regional, or customer-specific requirements.
